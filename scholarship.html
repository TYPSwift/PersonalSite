AAICS&YP Scholarship

Empowering students to achieve their short-term dreams.

About the Scholarship

The Amogh Allani Insolvent College Students and Young Professionals Scholarship offers a $15 micro-grant to support students and recent graduates in pursuing short-term wants.

Eligibility

  • Recent graduate or undergraduate student.

How to Apply

Submit the following by December 15, 2025:

  • Completed application form.
  • 15-word personal essay.
  • One letter of recommendation.
